Apple Product Launch Tweets (2020) 250.4mb

1.2 million tweets from 9/1/2020 to 12/1/2020. This was an interesting period of time because Apple had 3 major product launch events: Time Flies / Hi, Speed / One More Thing. All tweets over the previously mentioned period containing the following search terms were collected: "IPhone 12", "Apple Silicon", "M1 Macbook", "New Macbook", "IPhone 12 Pro", "New Apple", "New IPhone", "Silicon". The data was collected using twint.

Features: date, timezone, user_id, (user)name, location, tweet, reply count, retweet count, like count, and search term.
